# Wallet Health MiniApp

Wallet Health is a front-end utility that helps Neo users review wallet readiness, balances, and safety hygiene.
It stores checklist state locally on the device and does not require a smart contract.

## Features
- Neo N3 connection status
- NEO/GAS balance check
- Safety checklist with local persistence
- Risk level and actionable recommendations

## Usage
1. Connect your wallet and confirm you are on Neo N3.
2. Review balances and keep GAS for fees.
3. Complete the safety checklist and track your score.
4. Revisit regularly to maintain wallet health.
